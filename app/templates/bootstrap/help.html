Frequently Asked Questions

To create an RFQ:

  1. Navigate to the RFQ section
  2. Click "Create New RFQ"
  3. Fill in the required information including product category, quantity, and delivery date
  4. Add any special requirements
  5. Submit your RFQ

Suppliers will be notified and can submit quotes for your request.

You can track your orders in several ways:

  • Go to the Orders section to view all your orders
  • Use the search and filter options to find specific orders
  • Click on any order to view detailed information including status updates
  • Set up notifications to receive updates via email

We accept various payment methods:

  • Credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Bank transfers
  • Wire transfers
  • Payment terms for approved buyers

Payment terms and methods may vary by supplier and order value.

To become a supplier:

  1. Click on "Become a Supplier" in the navigation
  2. Fill out the supplier application form
  3. Provide required documentation (business license, certifications, etc.)
  4. Complete the verification process
  5. Set up your supplier profile and product catalog

Our team will review your application and contact you within 2-3 business days.

To cancel an order:

  1. Go to the Orders section
  2. Find the order you want to cancel
  3. Click on the order to view details
  4. Click the "Cancel Order" button
  5. Confirm the cancellation

Note: Orders can only be cancelled if they haven't been shipped yet. Cancellation policies may vary by supplier.
